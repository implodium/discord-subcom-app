.subcom settings <setting> <?method> <?change>

**setting: **

**prefix: **
- no method is needed. with no change supplied it will display the current prefix. With change supplied
it will change the prefix to the supplied prefix

**role: **
This setting has two methods.

- **set <@role> <count>: ** this method will give the @role a specific amount of (<count>) subcommunities to create
- **delete <@role>: ** this method will remove every permission of the role (set it to 0)

Is no method given it will tell you the already set permissions

**admin-role: **

- no method is needed. with no change supplied it will display the current admin-role. With change supplied
it will change the admin-role to the supplied admin-role
